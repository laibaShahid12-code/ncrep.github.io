Robbery is the unlawful taking of property from another by force or threat of force. Without force or threat of force, robbery has not occurred. Theft is a broad, catchall term that includes all crimes of stealing, plus burglary and robbery.



Mobile Snatching is a form of theft where criminals steal mobile phones from individuals, often through acts of physical confrontation, intimidation, or stealth.




Vandalism is the destruction of someone else's property. Some people think of graffiti as public art, while others dismiss it as nothing but vandalism. If you damage public or private property on purpose, you've committed the crime of vandalism.



Fraud is deliberately deceitful, dishonest, or untrue. Misrepresentation of a material fact. Made knowingly and with the intent to deceive. Reliance on the misrepresentation by the victim. Resulting in injury or damage from such reliance.
